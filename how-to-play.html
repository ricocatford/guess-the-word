How to play

Giving you a hint of the word, you have to guess it. There's thousands of words! Test your vocabulary with this wonderful game or challenge your friends for a match.

Rules

  • Guess the missing characters of the word.
  • There's no rounds limit.
  • There's no time limit.

Game Mechanics

  • At the beginning of the turn, a hint will be displayed.
  • Guess half of the word's missing characters.
  • Once you submit your answer, the result will be displayed including the complete word with its correspondent phonetic (if it's available).

Good to know

  • Game is case sensitive, do not use capital letters.
  • I've found some words that can be in plural form, keep this in mind.
  • I strongly recommend you also read the disclaimer before you play for first time.
