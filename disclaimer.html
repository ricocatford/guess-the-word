Powered by two APIs

This game is powered by two free APIs: Random Words API and Dictionary API. Being said that, I don't take responsibility for all the content of the words and definitions given, they are retrieved from both APIs and there's thousands of words.
I advice you are 18+ years old for playing the game as it might contain definitions that you could find 'sensitive'.
Game relies on these both APIs to work, it can stop working anytime if any of these two APIs change or go offline, I will regularly check on it.

Made for educational purposes

I've created Guess the Word: The Game for learning JavaScript and working with APIs. I also find it very educational for the player too, you can learn hundreds of words, grammar and phonetics. Challenge your friends for a 10 rounds game, It's much fun!
